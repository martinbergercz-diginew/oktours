Before You Travel

Everything you need to know before your trip

Safe and smooth travel starts with good preparation. We provide an overview of key information to help you prepare for every business trip.

🛂

Travel Documents

Passport validity, visas, business trip confirmations. We will notify you in advance of everything required for entry to your destination.

🔒

Security Situation

We continuously monitor security conditions in destination countries and alert you to any risks or travel advisories from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

🏥

Health & Insurance

International health insurance, vaccination requirements, and medical recommendations for specific destinations.

💱

Local Currency & Finance

Information on payment customs, exchange rates, and recommendations for safe handling of finances abroad.

📋

Corporate Travel Policy

We help establish and enforce internal travel rules — approval workflows, ticket categories, hotel categories.

Climate & Season

Information on weather, dress codes, and other practical tips for a comfortable stay at your destination.
